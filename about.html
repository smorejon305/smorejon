About Me

Hello! I'm a freelance web designer and developer from Miami, Florida, and currently living in Prague, in the Czech Republic.

My background is in linguistics and education. As a lifelong lover of languages and a language teacher for 15 years, helping people communicate effectively has always been a passion of mine. For as long as I can remember, I've been fascinated by the unique communities and cultures that languages seek to express. Web design and web development are another facet of this fascination, the perfect union of creativity and technology, and yet another language to communicate who we are and what our place is in the world.

Through elegant, visually captivating design and thoughtful user interfaces, my goal is first and foremost to ensure that my clients are successfully conveying their unique message to their target audience.

I'm always on the lookout for new and interesting projects, so please don't hesitate to reach out if you have a great idea and need some help getting it off the ground!
