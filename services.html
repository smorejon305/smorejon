Web
Design

UI Design
UI Design is the visual look and feel of your site. I'll work with you to create a design that reflects your brand and makes a great first impression. We'll choose the right colors, fonts, and images to give your website the look you want.
UX Design
I want to make sure that your website is easy to use and navigate. That's why I offer UX design services to create a seamless and enjoyable user experience for your visitors.
Responsive Design
With more and more people accessing websites on their mobile devices, it's important that your website looks great on any screen size. I make sure your website is optimized for all devices, so it looks just as good on a phone as it does on a desktop.
Site Audits
Not attracting as many customers as you'd like? I analyze your website's performance, usability, and security and provide you with recommendations to improve your site's overall performance.

Visual
Identity

Logo Design
Your logo is often the first thing people will see when they come across your brand, so it's important that it makes a great impression. I'll design a logo that represents your brand and is memorable and easily recognizable.
Visual Identity Design
I'll work with you to create a visual representation of your brand that encompasses everything from your logo and color palette to your typography and imagery. I'll make sure that your visual identity is unique and consistent across your website, socials, and all your marketing materials.
Style Guides
I'll create a style guide that outlines the visual elements of your brand identity, including logo usage guidelines, typography, and color palettes. This will help ensure consistency and cohesion across all of your marketing materials.

Web
Development

Website Builds
I'll build a customized website for your business or organization that meets your specific needs and goals. Whether you're starting from scratch or looking to refresh an existing site, I can help you create a website that stands out.
SEO & Analytics
I want your website to be seen by as many people as possible. That's why I offer SEO services to make sure your website is optimized for search engines, so it ranks higher in search results and attracts more traffic. I'll work with you to develop a strategy for improving your website's visibility and help you make data-driven decisions to improve your online presence.
Website Maintenance
I'll keep your website up-to-date and running smoothly with regular maintenance and updates. This includes things like software updates, security checks, and backups, so you can have peace of mind knowing your website is secure and stable.
CMS Integration
I can integrate a content management system (CMS) into your website, which will allow you to easily update and manage your website's content on your own. I'll work with you to choose the right CMS for your needs and provide training and support to help you get the most out of your new CMS.
E-Commerce Integration
If you're looking to sell products or services online, I can help you set up an e-commerce platform, such as Shopify, to make the process easy and seamless.

Platform Migration
If you're moving your website to a new platform, domain, or hosting provider, I can help you migrate your site with minimal disruption to your business. I'll take care of all the technical details and ensure that your website remains up and running throughout the migration process.
